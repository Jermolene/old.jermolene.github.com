Documentation Style Guide

22 days ago

The documentation for TiddlyWiki tries to follow the following guidelines. The aim is to ensure that documentation from different authors reads consistently.

  1. Do address the reader directly as "you"
  2. Avoid the passive voice in tutorial and introductory material
  3. Do use British spellings in preference to US spellings

See also the guidelines for tiddler titles.
